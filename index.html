How it Works

BuildInfer analyzes your project's build process to find optimization opportunities.

1. Record

BuildInfer executes your build to track its dependency graph.

2. Analyze

BuildInfer analyzes the dependency graph to extract high-level information about your build process.

3. Transpile

BuildInfer uses its analysis to generate optimized build scripts for your project.
